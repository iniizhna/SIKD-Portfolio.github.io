01 — About

Turning data into
accessible infrastructure

I am a researcher at National Central University, Taiwan, focusing on transportation accessibility and infrastructure measurement. My work bridges civil engineering, data science, and inclusive design to make urban environments more navigable for everyone.

My current research involves developing the Wheelchair Pathway Roughness Index (WPRI) — a metric for quantifying sidewalk surface quality using mobile inspection vehicles and sensor fusion techniques.
